ConnectWise PSA
Founded 1982 · Tampa, FL · ~40,000+ MSP partners · formerly ConnectWise Manage
Overview
SectionDetails
OverviewThe 800-lb gorilla of the MSP PSA market. Comprehensive platform with deep ecosystem of integrations (Automate RMM, Sell, Control). Acquired by Thoma Bravo (PE) in 2019. Known for breadth but also complexity, aggressive contracts, and slow innovation.
Target MarketSMB to mid-market MSPs (sweet spot: 15–100+ technicians), primarily managed IT focused
